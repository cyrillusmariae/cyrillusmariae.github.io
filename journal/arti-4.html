Popes celebrating the "Ad Orientem" Mass

Here are the few pictures and videos of Popes celebrating the Novus Ordo aka Ordinary Form of the Roman Rite in Ad Orientem. If you know of any other pictures or videos, please let me know!

The first picture is the First Mass of Pope St. John Paul II after he was elected Pope in the Sistine Chapel, I heard someone said that, Pope St. John Paul II always celebrated the Holy Mass in Ad Orientem inside his private chapel.

Another one is Pope Benedict XVI celebrated the Holy Mass in Ad Orientem on 13th January 2008 in the Sistine Chapel on the Feast of the Baptism of the Lord.

Then Pope Francis celebrated the Holy Mass in Ad Orientem on 18th May 2020 inside the Altar of St. Sebastian for the 100th Anniversary of the Birth of Pope St. John Paul II.
