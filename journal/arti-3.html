Some major renovation works on my house

It has been a while since my last post on this online journal.

Recently, my house needed to undergo major renovation works as ordered by the Government. This included removing the large window in my living room and the steel canopies outside both my room and my grandmother's room

There are some interesting facts about my house, especially concerning the large windows in my living room. About 50 years ago, there was a large balcony in my living room, but it was removed around the time I was born. The reason for removing the balcony was that the Government deemed it an 'illegal structure' and required its removal.

So the renovation work will take around 2 weeks, I'll update it when the works are finished!
