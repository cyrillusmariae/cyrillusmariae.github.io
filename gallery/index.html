22/6/2024 Thanksgiving Mass of João and Isabel Lau's 50th wedding anniversary

On June 22, 2024, I had the great privilege of serving as the Master of Ceremonies for the Holy Mass of Thanksgiving, by invitation from João and Isabel Lau, local parishioners in Macau. The Holy Mass took place in the School Chapel of Colégio Yuet Wah, Macau.
