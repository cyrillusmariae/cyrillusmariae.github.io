Summorum Ponfiticum Press is a small independent publishing studio based in Hong Kong, our aim is to preserving the sacred traditions and divine truths of the Roman Catholic Church.

Our publishing projects are including the Order of Holy Mass (Both Novus Ordo and Tridentine Rite), and some ceremonial books used in various liturgies.

The founder of Summorum Pontificum Press is Mr. Cyrillus Mariae Poon, he is an experienced Master of Ceremonies and an expert on Latin Rite Liturgies for more than 10 years.
