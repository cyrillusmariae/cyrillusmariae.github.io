D. Cyrillus Mariae C. Poon 潘朗希 (諾仁)

Born on June 14, 2003 in Hong Kong. He was baptized as a Roman Catholic in April 2012 by a Priest of the Prelature of Opus Dei.

His childish imagination is divided between historical Church buildings, traditional liturgies and the sacred arts.

He is an experienced Acolyte and Master of Ceremonies, and he has served in different parishes and communities in the past 10 years. Now he usually serve the Holy Mass and various liturgies as Master of Ceremonies by any invitations and appointments.

For some reason, he started exploring the Latin liturgical rites in the Catholic Church, and now he is interested in the Tridentine Rite, Novus Ordo, Ambrosian Rite, Mozarabic Rite and Lyonese Rite.

He is a member of the Confraternity of St. Peter (Abbreviation: C.S.P), which is a sodality of members who wish to unite themselves to the Priestly Fraternity of St. Peter and aid in the work of our fraternity, primarily by their prayers.

In his free time, Chavez loves designing liturgical books such as the Order of Holy Mass for different Latin liturgical rites and other kinds of books for liturgical use.

 

 

Greetings and Welcome!

Today, more than ever before, we're facing the great challenges of lost identity and of abandoning the fundamental values of Christian morality such as the family integrity, the faith and love for God and for each other.

As Catholic Christians we carry the important responsibility of safeguarding the spiritual heritage Jesus has left to us incorporated in these three basic principles:

1. Preserving of life

2. Contemplating and spreading the Holy Gospel

3. Being one with Jesus through the Holy Eucharist.

I wish you that your journey here will provide to you the spiritual foundations to find the perfect life balance and make you to an instrument of our Lord's peace and love.

God Bless You!
