D. Cyrillus Mariae C. Poon CSP

Born on June 14, 2003 in Hong Kong, he was baptized as a Roman Catholic in April 2012 by a Priest of the Prelature of Opus Dei.

His childhood His imagination is captivated by historical Church buildings, traditional liturgies, and sacred arts.

He is an experienced Acolyte and Master of Ceremonies, having served in various parishes and communities over the past 10 years. Currently, he serves at the Holy Mass and other liturgies as a Master of Ceremonies through invitations and appointments.

For reasons unknown, he began exploring the Latin liturgical rites in the Catholic Church and has developed a keen interest in the Tridentine Rite, Novus Ordo, Ambrosian Rite, Mozarabic Rite, and Lyonese Rite.

He is a member of the Confraternity of St. Peter (Abbreviation: C.S.P), a sodality of individuals who seek to align themselves with the Priestly Fraternity of St. Peter and support the fraternity primarily through their prayers.

In his leisure time, Chavez enjoys designing liturgical books such as the Order of Holy Mass for various Latin liturgical rites and other books for liturgical purposes.

 

 

Greetings and Welcome!

 Today, more than ever before, we're facing the great challenges of lost identity and of abandoning the fundamental values of Christian morality such as the family integrity, the faith and love for God and for each other.

As Catholic Christians we carry the important responsibility of safeguarding the spiritual heritage Jesus has left to us incorporated in these three basic principles:

1. Preserving of Life

2. Comtemplating and Spreading the Holy Gospel

3. Being one with Jesus Christ through the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass

I wish you that your journey here will provide to you the spiritual foundations to find the perfect life balance and make you to an instrument of our Lord's peace and love.
