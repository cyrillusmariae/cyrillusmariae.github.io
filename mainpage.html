Greetings and Welcome!

Cyrillus Mariae C. Poon is a Traditional Roman Catholic Master of Ceremonies in Hong Kong and an expert in Western Latin Rite Liturgies within the Roman Catholic Church.

For the past 10 years, he has served as an Acolyte and Master of Ceremonies for various communities and parishes. Currently, he regularly serves Holy Mass and other liturgies upon any invitations and appointments.

Cyrillus is also a Member of the Confraternity of St. Peter (Latin: Confraternitas Sancti Petri / Abbrevation: C.S.P). It is a society which gathers those who feel close to the Priestly Fraternity of St. Peter and who wish to support its charism through prayers and sacrifices.

He can be contacted by E-mail at chavezpoon@icloud.com.
